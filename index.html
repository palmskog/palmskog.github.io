About me

I am a Research Fellow at the University of Texas at Austin. I was previously a Postdoctoral Research Associate and Visiting Scholar at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I obtained my Ph.D. in 2014 from KTH Royal Insitute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden, advised by Mads Dam. Earlier, I obtained my M.Sc. from KTH in Computer Science and Engineering.
