Karl Palmskog

α About me

I am a Research Fellow at The University of Texas at Austin, working in Milos Gligoric's group.

I was previously a Postdoctoral Research Associate and Visiting Scholar at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2014 from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden, advised by Mads Dam. Earlier, in 2007, I obtained my M.Sc. from KTH in Computer Science and Engineering.

See also my CV and GitHub profile.

β Research Interests

My interests span programming languages, formal methods, and software engineering.

In particular, I am interested in construction of efficient (distributed) software systems with formal guarantees of correctness, and development of proof engineering and testing techniques that make this possible. I am a frequent user of the Coq proof assistant for both proving and programming, usually complemented by a measure of OCaml.
